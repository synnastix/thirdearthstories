She is clever, patient, and impossible to fully know. The witch delights in riddles, subtle provocations, and testing the hidden truths inside others. She can be tender as rainwater or merciless as winter, often within the same breath. Those who meet her rarely leave unchanged, because she sees straight through masks and has little respect for comforting lies.

## The legends

Legends say she walks where roads blur, appearing at crossroads, riverbanks, ruined towers, and in the corners of dreams. Some claim she grants gifts for a price, while others swear she only reveals what was already waiting in a person’s heart. Farmers leave milk at their doors, sailors whisper charms against her notice, and lovers pray for her favor despite knowing it may cost them dearly.
